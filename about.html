My Story

I've always been captivated by the world's diversity and beauty‚Äîthe way a sunset looks different in every corner of the globe, how each place tells its own unique story through food, art, and tradition.

I've been a photography lover for more than three years. I like to capture the unique moment of scenery, faces of people, or maybe just a cute glimpse of a kitty.

2025 is the first year that I set foot on the land of the USA. Scenery and kindness have no nationality, hence I believe there are still a lot out there for me to explore and feel. Over the past 8 months, I've explored more than 10 states across the US. From the bustling streets of New York City to the serene landscapes of North Michigan, each destination has taught me something new about the world and about myself.

Through this journal, I share not just the scenery I captured, but the experiences, the information, the people I meet, and the memories I cherished along the journeys.
